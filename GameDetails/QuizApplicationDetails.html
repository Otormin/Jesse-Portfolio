Project Overview

This is a fun browser-based quiz application that fetches random questions from the Open Trivia Database. Players answer 10 random questions and receive a score out of 100. After completing the quiz, they enter their name and their score is saved in local storage. The app then ranks all players saved in local storage based on their high scores.

Key Features

  • Fetches random questions from Open Trivia Database
  • Players answer 10 random questions
  • Score out of 100 shown at the end
  • Players enter their name after playing
  • Scores and names stored in local storage
  • Ranks all players by high scores
  • Responsive design for all devices
  • Simple, clean user interface
