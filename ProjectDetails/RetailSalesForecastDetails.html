Project Overview

This project leverages Facebook Prophet for time series forecasting to predict retail sales trends, integrated with interactive Power BI dashboards for data visualization and business intelligence insights. The solution helps retailers anticipate demand, manage inventory, and optimize business decisions.

Key Features

  • Time series forecasting with Prophet (trend & seasonality modeling)
  • Data preprocessing with Pandas and feature engineering
  • Interactive Power BI dashboards with drill-through and slicers
  • Business-ready insights for demand planning and inventory management
